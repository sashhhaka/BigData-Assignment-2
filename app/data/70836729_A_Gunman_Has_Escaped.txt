A Gunman Has Escaped is a 1948 British crime film directed by Richard M. Grey and starring John Harvey, Maria Charles and Jane Arden.Chibnall & McFarlane p.35 It was produced as a second feature and shot at the Maida Vale Studios in London. It was released by the independent distributor Monarch Film Corporation. ==Cast== * John Harvey as Eddie Steele * John Fitzgerald as Sinclair * Robert Cartland as Bill Grant * Ernest Brightmore as Johnson * Maria Charles as Goldie * Patrick Westwood as Red * George Self as Spike * Manville Tarrant as Alf * Denis Lehrer as Mike * Jane Arden as Jane * Frank Hawkins as Mr. Cranston * Hope Carr as Mrs. Cranston * Melville Crawford as Inspector Fenton * Hatton Duprez as Detective ==References== ==Bibliography== * Chibnall, Steve & McFarlane, Brian.
