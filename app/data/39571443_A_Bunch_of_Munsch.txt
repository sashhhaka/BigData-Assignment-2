The show aired from December 7, 1991 to December 2, 1992 on CTV in Canada and Showtime in the United States. All episodes were released to VHS by Golden Book Video in the early 1990s. The releases were eventually packaged and updated in the late 1990s by Sony Wonder. Originally, there was going to be an episode based on Love You Forever, but due to budget cuts, it was scrapped.
