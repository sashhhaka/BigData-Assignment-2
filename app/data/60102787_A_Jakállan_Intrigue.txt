thumb|upright|right A Jakállan Intrigue is a 1984 fantasy role-playing game adventure published by Tékumel Games for Empire of the Petal Throne and Swords & Glory. ==Contents== A Jakállan Intrigue is an adventure in which player characters act in conjunction with several non-player characters acting as guards, assistants, etc., in the city of Jakalla. ==Reception== Frederick Paul Kiesche III reviewed A Jakállan Intrigue in Space Gamer No. 71. Kiesche commented that "Intrigue is an excellent adventure, far superior to much of what is currently on the market. A fine new product from Tekumel Games, indicating that we have real talent working there!" ==References== Category:Fantasy role-playing game adventures Category:Role-playing game supplements introduced in 1984 Category:Tékumel
