A Blonde for a Night is a 1928 American silent comedy film directed by E. Mason Hopper and F. McGrew Willis and starring Marie Prevost, Franklin Pangborn and Harrison Ford.Munden p.68 The film's sets were designed by the art director Stephen Goosson. ==Premise== After an argument with her husband on their honeymoon in Paris, a wife disguises herself as a blonde woman to test her husband's fidelity. ==Cast== * Marie Prevost as Marcia Webster * Franklin Pangborn as Hector * Harrison Ford as Bob Webster * T.
