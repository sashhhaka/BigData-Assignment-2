A Collection of Romantic Themes is a compilation album by keyboardist Yanni, released on the Private Music label in 1994. This compilation consists of tracks from his 1992 Grammy-nominated album Dare to Dream and his 1993 follow- up album In My Time as well as from Reflections of Passion and Live at the Acropolis. This album is no longer in print. ==Track listing== ==Production== * Engineered and mixed by Yanni at his private studios *Mastered at Bernie Grundman Mastering, Los Angeles * Produced and Manufactured exclusively for Dayton Hudson.
