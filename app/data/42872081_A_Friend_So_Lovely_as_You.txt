A Friend So Lovely as You (German: ) is an operetta by Will Meisel to a libretto by Willy Rosen and Marcel Lion. It premiered at the on 19 September 1930.Grange p. 343 The same year, it was turned into a film with Anny Ondra, Felix Bressart and Adele Sandrock, directed by Carl Lamac; the title song was performed by Zarah Leander. ==References== ==Bibliography== * Grange, William.
