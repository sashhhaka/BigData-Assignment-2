A Country Called Home is a 2015 American drama film directed by Anna Axster and starring Imogen Poots, Mackenzie Davis, Ryan Bingham, Mary McCormack and June Squibb. It is Axster's feature directorial debut. ==Cast== *Imogen Poots *Mary McCormack *Mackenzie Davis *Ryan Bingham *June Squibb *Shea Whigham *Josh Helman *Norman Bennett ==Release== The film premiered at the 2015 LA Film Festival. It was then released in theaters on February 26, 2016 and on VOD on March 1, 2016. ==Reception== The film has a 17% rating on Rotten Tomatoes. Mark Dujsik of RogerEbert.com awarded the film two and a half stars. Jesse Hassenger of The A.V. Club graded the film a C.
