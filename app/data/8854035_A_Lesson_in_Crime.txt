A Lesson in Crime is an EP by Tokyo Police Club released April 27, 2006. It reached #194 on the Canadian album charts. The single "Nature of the Experiment" is featured as the opening to the Comedy Central sketch comedy show Nick Swardson's Pretend Time. ==Track listing== All songs written by Alsop, Hooks, Monks, and Wright. == Personnel == * Greg Alsop – drums * Joshua Hook – guitar * Graham Wright – keyboards, vocals * David Monks – vocals, bass guitar * Jon Drew – producer, engineering, tambourine * Rich Cohen – management * Geoff Wilson – artwork ==Charts== Chart (2007) Peak position ==References== Category:2006 debut EPs Category:Tokyo Police Club albums Category:Paper Bag Records EPs
