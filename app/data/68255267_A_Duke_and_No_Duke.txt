A Duke and No Duke is a 1684 comedy play by the Irish writer Nahum Tate.Canfield p.185 It was originally staged by the United Company at the Drury Lane Theatre in London with a cast including John Wiltshire as Lavinio, Thomas Gillow as Barberino, Joseph Williams as Alberto, Anthony Leigh as Trappolin, Thomas Percival as Mago, Elizabeth Currer as Isabella and Susannah Percival as Prudentia.Van Lennep p.328 ==References== ==Bibliography== * Canfield, J.
