"A Little Bit of Heaven", is a 1965 hit song recorded by Ronnie Dove. ==Background== The single was released by Diamond Records. The song peaked at #16 on the Hot 100 and #4 on the Easy Listening Chart. It was also included in his album One Kiss for Old Times' Sake. == Chart performance == Chart (1965) Peak position U.S. Billboard Hot 100 16 U.S. Billboard Easy Listening 4 == Cover Versions == Singer Wayne Newton covered the song for his "Summer Wind" album. ==References== Category:1965 singles Category:Ronnie Dove songs Category:Songs written by Kenny Young Category:1965 songs Category:Songs written by Artie Resnick
