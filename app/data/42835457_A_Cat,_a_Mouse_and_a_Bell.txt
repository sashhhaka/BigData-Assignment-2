A Cat, a Mouse and a Bell is a 1935 American animated short film, part of the Color Rhapsodies series. ==Summary== Sniffles the mouse and his friend "The Book Worm" are harassed by a cat. They figure that by belling the cat (partly true) they could hear him coming and eliminate the element of surprise.
