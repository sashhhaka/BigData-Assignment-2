A Circuit Rider's Wife is a 1910 novel by Corra May Harris that is thought to be largely autobiographical, Circuit rider here meaning a travelling clergyman. It was published by Henry Altemus Company in Philadelphia.A Circuit Rider's Wife, the book freely available The book is Harris's best known work.
