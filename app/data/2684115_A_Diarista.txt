A Diarista (in Portuguese, literally "The Daily Help") is a Brazilian television sitcom directed by José Alvarenga Jr. and written by Aloísio de Abreu and Bruno Mazzeo. The series aired from 13 April 2004 to 31 July 2007 on Rede Globo. ==Plot== It shows the life and problems of Marinete (Cláudia Rodrigues), a cleaning lady with a short temper.
