A Hungry Heart is a 1917 silent film drama directed by Emile Chautard and starring Alice Brady. It was produced and distributed by World Film Corporation.A Hungry Heart at silentera.comThe AFI Catalog of Feature Films:A Hungry Heart It is sometimes called The Hungry Heart but shouldn't be confused with the Pauline Frederick film of the same year.
