A Gamble for Love is a 1917 British silent sports film directed by Frank Wilson and starring Gerald Ames, James Lindsay and George Foley.Low p.182 It was based on the 1914 novel of the same title by Nat Gould, set in the horseracing world. ==Cast== * Gerald Ames as Dennis Laurenny * James Lindsay as Lord Ingleby * Arthur Walcott as Joe Rothey * Hubert Willis as Bryhynz * Violet Hopson as Fay de Launay * George Foley * John MacAndrews * J.
