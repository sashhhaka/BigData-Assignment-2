A Gentleman of Quality is a 1919 American silent drama film directed by James Young and starring Earle Williams, Kathryn Adams, Joyce Moore, James Carpenter, and Robert Bolder. It is based on the 1909 novel of the same name by Frederick Van Rensselaer Dey. The film was released by Vitagraph Company of America on March 17, 1919. ==Plot== ==Cast== *Earle Williams as John Ashton / Lord John Hartford *Kathryn Adams as Lady Mercy Covington *Joyce Moore as Hope Hollister *James Carpenter as Harry Hollister *Robert Bolder as Robert *George C.
