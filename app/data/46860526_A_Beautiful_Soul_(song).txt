"A Beautiful Soul" is a power ballad by Poison lead singer Bret Michaels, and was released as the lead single for the album True Grit. The song was released as a single on April 1, 2014 over one year before the release of the album, which was released May 5, 2015. Retrieved August 24, 2021. His oldest daughter Raine makes a cameo appearance in the video. The video has generated over 1.2 million views in its first seven days.
