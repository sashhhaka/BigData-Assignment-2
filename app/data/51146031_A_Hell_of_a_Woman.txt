A Hell of a Woman is a 1954 novel by Jim Thompson. It has been adapted for the screen by Alain Corneau and Georges Pérec as Série noire, released in 1979. ==Plot== Frank "Dolly" Dillon hates his job of working collections for Pay-E- Zee Stores. He loathes his wife, Joyce, and has an account balance that barely lets him pay the bills each month. Working door-to-door one day, Dolly crosses paths with a beautiful young woman, Mona who is being forced by her aunt to do things that she does not want to with men she does not know.
