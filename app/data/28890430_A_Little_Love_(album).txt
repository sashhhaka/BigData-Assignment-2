A Little Love is the debut studio album by Hong Kong-based artist Fiona Fung, under the label Sony Music. The album has two editions. The CD + DVD edition was released on 20 November 2008. It features three music videos and a video of Fiona's Tokyo trip. Another edition, in Blu-spec CD format, was subsequently released on 13 January 2010.
