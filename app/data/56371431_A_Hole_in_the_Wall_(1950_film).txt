A Hole in the Wall (French: Un trou dans le mur) is a 1950 French comedy film directed by Émile Couzinet and starring André Alerme, Marguerite Pierry and Raymond Galle. It is based on a play by Yves Mirande which had previously been made into a 1930 film of the same title.Goble p.328 ==Cast== * André Alerme as Campignac * Marguerite Pierry as Artémise Corbin * Raymond Galle as André de Kerdrec * Jacqueline Dor as Lucie * Nina Myral as La concierge * Gaby Basset as La cliente * François Joux as Anatole * Palau as L'antiquaire * Claudette Falco as La secrétaire == References == == Bibliography == * Goble, Alan.
