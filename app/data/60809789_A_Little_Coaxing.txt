A Little Coaxing () is an oil painting by the French artist William-Adolphe Bouguereau, painted and completed in 1890, and now owned by a private collector. It depicts two barefooted sisters, the older girl sitting on a concrete step and the younger sister giving her a kiss on the cheek. ==References== Category:Paintings by William-Adolphe Bouguereau Category:1890 paintings Category:Portraits of women Category:Children in art Category:Allegorical paintings by French artists Category:19th-century allegorical paintings
