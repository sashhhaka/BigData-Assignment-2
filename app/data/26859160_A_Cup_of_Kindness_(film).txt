A Cup of Kindness is a 1934 British comedy film directed by and starring Tom Walls. It also featured Ralph Lynn, Robertson Hare, Dorothy Hyson and Claude Hulbert.BFI.org It was based on a 1929 play by Ben Travers of the same name, one of the Aldwych farces, and had four of the same cast members.
