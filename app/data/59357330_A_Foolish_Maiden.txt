A Foolish Maiden (French: La vierge folle) is a 1929 French silent comedy film directed by Luitz-Morat and starring Emmy Lynn, Jean Angelo and Suzy Vernon.Crisp p.121 The film's sets were designed by the art director Pierre Schild. ==Cast== * Emmy Lynn as Fanny Armaury * Jean Angelo as Armaury * Suzy Vernon as Diane de Charance * Maurice Schutz as Le duc de Charance * Pierre Fresnay as Gaston de Charance * Simone Judic as Kerty * Marguerite de Morlaye as La duchesse du Charance ==References== ==Bibliography== * Crisp, Colin.
